Build discipline, awareness, and efficiency with DOLPTIPID!

Doltipid is a budget monitoring website, designed to help Senior High School students manage and track their weekly expenses in food, transportation, and savings. The goal is to promote financial literacy, disciplines, and smart budgeting habits among students.

Optional Tips

Budget Rule: 60 / 30 / 10

Use the 60-30-10 rule to manage your money wisely.

60% - Needs

Essentials: Bills, Rent, Groceries, Transport

30% - Wants

Leisure: Food, Shopping, Subscriptions

10% - Savings

Emergency Fund, Investments, or Goals

Saving Tips

  • Track your spending weekly so you can see where your money goes.
  • Pay yourself first by automatically saving a portion of your income.
  • Set a weekly spending limit to control impulsive purchases.
  • Build an emergency fund of at least 3–6 months of expenses.
  • Use the 24-hour rule to avoid unnecessary wants.
